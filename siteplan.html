Purpose of the Site

The purpose of this site is to educate and entertain users interested in chess. It will provide clear information about its history, analyze master moves performed by champions, and teach the basic rules for those who want to learn how to play.

Scenarios

  • What is the origin of chess and how has it evolved over time?
  • What famous moves have champions like Magnus Carlsen or Bobby Fischer made?
  • Where can I learn how to move the pieces and play a full game?

Color Scheme

  • Dark Purple (#4b0082): for headers and highlighted buttons.
  • Light Beige (#f5f5dc): as the site background to evoke a classic chessboard feel.
  • Black (#000000): for main text and important details.

Typography

  • Verdana: for the body text due to its clarity and readability.
  • Georgia: for titles due to its elegant and classic style.
