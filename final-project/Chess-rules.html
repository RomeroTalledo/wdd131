Piece Symbol Movement Special Notes
King ♔ One square in any direction Can castle with a rook if not moved
Queen ♕ Any number of squares in any direction Most powerful piece
Rook ♖ Horizontally or vertically Can castle with the king
Bishop ♗ Diagonally Stays on the same color square
Knight ♘ L-shape: 2 squares in one direction, then 1 square perpendicular Can jump over other pieces
Pawn ♙ One square forward (two on first move), captures diagonally Can promote when reaching the 8th rank
